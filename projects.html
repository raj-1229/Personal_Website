Resume Scanning

Our approach requires the submission of a resume from the can- didate. The submitted resume is screened for skills during the screening process.

Resume Matching

The Skills extracted from the resume of a candidate and the Job Description document are used to generate a matching resume score.

Question Generation

The short answer questions are generated using questgen from the paragraph corresponding to extracted skills.

Answer Evaluation

Calculate the cosine distance between the saved answer and the candidate’s answer and generate BERT embeddings. The greater the distance, the less likely the match.

Proctoring

During the interviewing process, proctoring will be enabled in order to ensure that malpractices are prevented.
